About CargoCloud

CargoCloud Transport & Moving provides professional trucking, freight, and moving services across the United States. We combine experienced CDL drivers, modern equipment, and responsive dispatch to deliver reliable service on every job.

Our mission is to provide safe, on-time, and transparent transportation and moving solutions that businesses and families can depend on. From single loads to recurring lanes, we treat every shipment like it matters—because it does.
