About CargoCloud

CargoCloud Transport & Moving provides professional trucking, freight, and moving services across the United States. We combine experienced CDL drivers, modern equipment, and responsive dispatch to deliver reliable service on every job.

Our mission is to provide safe, on-time, and transparent transportation and moving solutions that businesses and families can depend on. From single loads to recurring lanes, we treat every shipment like it matters—because it does.

Our Core Values

Reliability: We deliver as promised, with clear ETAs and proactive communication.

Safety: Strict safety standards for drivers, equipment, and cargo handling.

Transparency: Clear pricing, no hidden fees, and honest updates.

Customer Focus: Routes, schedules, and services tailored to your needs.

Services Built for Real-World Logistics

From full truckloads to local moves, we cover the lanes and loads that keep your business moving.

FTL

Full Truckload (FTL) Shipping

Dedicated truck capacity for large shipments requiring fast, secure, point-to-point delivery.

LTL

Less-Than-Truckload (LTL)

Cost-efficient option for smaller shipments that don’t require a full trailer.

Flatbed

Flatbed Hauling

Safe transport for oversized, irregular, or heavy cargo such as machinery and construction materials.

Reefer

Refrigerated Transport

Temperature-controlled shipping for food, beverages, pharmaceuticals, and other sensitive goods.

Express

Expedited Freight

Priority routing and fast delivery for time-critical shipments and high-value cargo.

Moving

Residential & Commercial Moving

Professional moving services for homes and businesses, handled with care and planning.
